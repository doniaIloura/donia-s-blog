Jelly Cubes

    Summary
    The epic love story centered on eight Zulu men, whose surname is Zulu, continues, Naledi his Love, focuses on Qhawe and the woman he comes to love, Naledi. She like Hlomu and Zandile comes to understand that loving one of the Zulu brothers means loving all of his dark and tumultuous past and as much as she will enjoy the trappings that comes with being the wife of one of those rich, handsome, powerful and dangerous men. she also has to accept and live with the inherent danger that remains. This is the third novel in the Hlomu series
