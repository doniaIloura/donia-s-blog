Hlomu the wife

    Summary
    Hlomu the wife is about essentially a love story that takes its cues from life in south African township . A young women named Mahlomu meets Mghele Zulu and they fall in love
    Even though aspects of Mghele's personality and past make her uncomfortable, Hlomu is happy. their love is strong and they stand by each other through good and bad.
    But Mghele and his seven brothers have a dark and tumultuous past that involves a dead warlord father, mob justice, and a lots of unaccounted-for money. 
    The Zulu brothers are rich, powerful and dangerous . They enventually become one of the wealthiest and most powerful famiels in johannesburg- but the inherit danger remain
